## 💡 Quick Tips for Discrete Maths
1. **Focus on Proofs**: Discrete maths relies heavily on logical reasoning. Make sure you don't just memorize formulas, but understand *why* they work (especially induction and contradiction).
2. **Visualize**: Use Venn diagrams for sets, Hasse diagrams for posets, truth tables for logic, and digraphs/trees for graph theory.
3. **Practice Active Recall**: Create flashcards or quick summaries of key theorems (e.g., Fermat's Little Theorem, Pigeonhole Principle, Handshaking Lemma).
