## 📖 Resource Overview

This PDF contains pages 58–65 from C. L. Liu's classic textbook *Elements of Discrete Mathematics*, focusing on **Computability and Formal Languages** (Russell's Paradox, Ordered Sets, and Formal Languages).

---

## 🗺️ Mapping of Study Notes

These pages serve as the primary reference for the following topics in **Chapter 01: Sets, Relations and Functions**:
* **Russell's Paradox & Noncomputability**: Bertrand Russell's naive set theory paradox and its computational link to the Halting Problem (see [[30. Russells Paradox and Noncomputability]]).
* **Ordered Sets ($n$-tuples)**: The recursive construction of ordered pairs and $n$-tuples (see [[04. Cartesian Products]]).
* **Formal Languages**: Definitions of alphabets, words, Kleene Star ($A^*$), and languages as subsets (see [[31. Formal Languages and the Kleene Star]]).
